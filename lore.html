🔥 The Legend of Fire Dragon

Forged in the heart of sleeping volcanoes, the Fire Dragon commands flame and fury. It is said their breath can melt stone and their gaze can ignite the air itself.

❄️ Ice Wyrm's Whisper

Hidden beneath ancient glaciers, the Ice Wyrm dreams in silence. Few have seen them and lived to tell the tale — their beauty is matched only by their deadly chill.

⚡ Storm Serpent Prophecy

These sky-born serpents ride the lightning and speak in thunder. Legends say they can split mountains with a scream and vanish in a flash of light.

🌿 The Forest Guardian

Gentle but powerful, this dragon protects the sacred groves. Trees bloom in its wake, but beware — disturb the forest, and the Guardian awakens.

🖤 Shadow Wyvern Curse

Born from forgotten magic and dark whispers, the Shadow Wyvern hides in eternal dusk. Its presence brings fear, its flight — silence. Some say it was never alive.
